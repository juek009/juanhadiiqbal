Personal Writing

What is Personal Writing?

Personal writing is a piece of writing that describes one's life experiences or events in an entertaining and informative way. Personal writing is a form of anecdotal (based on a personal experience) storytelling that frequently imparts a learning experience or knowledge and understanding to the audience.

Poem Analysis

Be Proud Of Who You Are By S Raine

This poem talks about being proud of your identity and also being yourself, as well as the fact that nobody is perfect. I like this poem because it reveals us that you can do and be whatever you want, and that what actually matters is how you feel about yourself, not what others think. It's also about how feeling hurt and crying is a normal aspect of being human, and how it's acceptable to be in such a way. The poem also emphasizes that size doesn't matter and that you should be confident in your own skin. I believe it is a very eye-opening and amazing poem because it discusses how you should feel regarding yourself and how you ought to hold yourself in the highest respect. This was written in considerable detail and exceptionally well by the poet, particularly since it rhymes. By reading the title, you can also explain what the poem is about.
